# Smart filters

## What are they?

Smart filters are our way of using technology to protect your mental health and well-being online.

It lessens the noise and cools down the temperature. 

It helps you feel better and makes taking a break just a bit easier.

## How do they work?

Smart filters are an advanced feature of ezfilter that uses a combination of heuristics and machine learning algorithms to provide more accurate and context-aware filtering capabilities. These filters can analyze patterns in data and make intelligent decisions about what content to allow, block, or modify based on learned behaviors and characteristics.
